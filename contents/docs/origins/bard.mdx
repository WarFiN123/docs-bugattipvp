# Features

- You deal more damage on low health
- You gain regeneration when a nearby note block is played
- You can duplicate allays without a jukebox playing
- You gain regeneration by right clicking with an amethyst shard
- Creepers you kill have a 25% chance of dropping music discs even if not killed by a skeleton
