# Features

- You have perfect aim with a bow
- Arrows you shoot deal more damage than ones shot by humans
- Arrows you shoot are faster than ones shot by humans
- Potion effects on your arrows are stronger
- By left clicking with your bow, you shoot 3 arrows for the cost of 1, though this disables your bow for 7 seconds
