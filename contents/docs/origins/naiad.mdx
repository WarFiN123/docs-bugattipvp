# Features

- You have extra health and deal more damage in water or rain
- You're weaker and slower when not in the water
- You can breathe underwater but not on land
- You can see clearly underwater
- You can break blocks underwater at the same speed as on land
- You swim faster in water than a human
- You only sink in water when you want to
