## /kits
- Shows all kits

## /rtp
- Teleports to random coordinates

## /shop
- Shows the shop

## /ah
- Shows the auction house

## /vote
- Vote for the server in Minecraft listings

## /deathback
- Teleports you back to your death location

## /clan
- Visit the [Clans](./clans) page for more information

## /balance
- Shows your balance

## /pay
- Pay someone
